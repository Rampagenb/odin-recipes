Sunset Sangria Recipe

Fruity, fresh Sangria Recipe

This tropical sangria is great at any party, or on a hot day! Pair it with a moist burger and crispy fries for a sweet, summer meal! Complete it with a juicy peach pie for a decadent finish

Ingredients

  • 1 (750ml) bottle rose wine
  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup vodka
  • 1/4 cup triple sec
  • 1/2 cup simple syrup
  • 1 orange, sliced into rounds
  • 1 lemon, sliced into rounds
  • 1 lime, sliced into rounds
  • 1 (6 ounce) container fresh raspberries

Preparation

  1. Combine wine, pineapple juice, vodka, triple sec, and simple syrup in a large bowl or pitcher. Refrigerate until chilled, about 1 hour.
  2. Stir the oranges, lemons, limes, and raspberries into the chilled sangria at least 30 minutes before serving. The longer the fruit marinates the better. Serve over ice.
